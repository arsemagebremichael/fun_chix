Our Mental Fitness

Our minds are like muscles. We train it, it keeps growing stronger; we don't, it gets flabby. Hence why we should be practicing our minds with reading, puzzles, meditation and acquiring new skills. It’s not just about acing trivia night (though that’s a bonus). Think of this like going to the gym, with more fun and gains. A practiced mind makes you a better friend, colleague, and human. It fuels curiosity, helping you connect with others through deeper conversations and fresh perspectives.
